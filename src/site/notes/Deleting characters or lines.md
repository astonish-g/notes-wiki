##### Info
Below, you will learn the technics and keybindings to delete characters or lines. 

##### In NORMAL mode:
- **Delete a character :** 
	1. **Move** the **cursor** until it is on **top** of the **character** to be **deleted**.
	2. **Press** the ==x== key to delete unwanted character.
