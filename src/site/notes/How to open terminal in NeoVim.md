##### Info
Sometimes, like when you are doing python scripts..etc, you may want to try your script on terminal. You can open terminal inside your Neovim.

##### How to do it?
###### To open vertically:
In **normal** mode:
- Press ==Alt + V==

###### To open horizontally:
In **normal** mode:
- Press ==Alt + H==
