##### Info:
Sometimes for a cleaner look, you may want to **toggle** the **line numbers**.

##### How to do it?
- While you are in **NORMAL** mode.
- Type **leader** + **n** 

You will see the line numbers dissappear. If you press it again, they will reappear.
