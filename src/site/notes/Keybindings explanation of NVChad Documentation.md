##### Info:
While reading the NVChad documentation, the way that they represent the **modifier keys** in their documentation can be quite confusing. So here's a simple explanation:

- ==leader key :== is the **space** key
- ==A :== is the **ALT** key
- ==C :== is the **CTRL** key
- ==S :== is the **SHIFT** key
