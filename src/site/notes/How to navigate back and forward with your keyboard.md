Sometimes you do **NOT want** to **show** the **top toolbar** but you can still navigate **back** and **forward** with **keyboard shortcuts**.

##### How to do it?
- Press ==CTRL + ALT + Left Arrow== to go ==back==.
- Press ==CTRL + ALT + Right Arrow== to go ==forward==.
